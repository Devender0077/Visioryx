# Visioryx — Mobile App Product Requirements ## Product Overview **Visioryx** is an AI-powered surveillance dashboard featuring live RTSP camera streams, face recognition, object detection, alerts, and analytics. ## Brand & UI Principles - **Name:** Visioryx - **Feel:** Professional security / enterprise SaaS — clean, high contrast, calm. - **Primary Accent:** Blue `#2065D1` - **Status Colors:** Success `#00AB55`, Warning `#FFAB00`, Danger `#FF5630` - **Mode:** Support light and dark modes. - **Accessibility:** WCAG AA contrast, touch targets ≥ 44pt. ## Key Features & Flows - **Auth:** JWT-based login, Splash screen. - **Home/Overview:** KPI cards for users, cameras, and detections. - **Live Monitoring:** Grid of camera streams + detail viewer. - **Management:** Camera and User lists (Admin/Operator roles). - **Alerts & Analytics:** Real-time event notifications and trend charts. - **Enrollment:** Multi-step photo upload process for face recognition. ## Technical Context - **Backend:** FastAPI with WebSocket support. - **Video:** MJPEG streams via RTSP. - **Navigation:** Role-based navigation (Admin vs. Operator vs. Enrollee).
