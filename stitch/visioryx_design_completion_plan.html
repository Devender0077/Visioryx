# Visioryx Design Audit & Completion Plan Based on the product requirements and current canvas, here is the plan to ensure full feature coverage and functional "CRUD" capabilities. ## 1. Missing Core Screens We need to design the following to reach MVP status: - **User Management:** List of enrolled users with search, roles, and face enrollment status. - **QR Enrollment:** The modal/screen for generating enrollment links for new users. - **Detailed Analytics:** Trends, charts for known vs. unknown detections, and object types. - **Audit Log (Admin):** A chronological record of system actions. ## 2. Functional "CRUD" Enhancements I will audit the existing screens to ensure these actions are intuitive: - **Cameras:** Ensure the "Add/Edit Camera" flow is fully visualized with RTSP masking and validation. - **Alerts:** Verify "Mark all read" and filtering functionality. - **Users:** Add the "Register User" and "Delete User" flows. ## 3. Role-Based Refinement - Ensure the **Admin Settings** (SMTP, Audit Logs) are clearly marked as restricted. - Verify that the **Operator** view hides these administrative functions. ## 4. Interaction & State Review - Add **Empty States** for when no cameras or alerts are present. - Define **Error States** for connection losses (MJPEG stream failures).
